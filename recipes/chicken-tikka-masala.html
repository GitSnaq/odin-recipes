Chicken Tikka Masala

Description of the dish

This dish is made using primarily chicken obviously and tomatoes.

Usually the chicken is marinated in lots of spices with yogurt which is then after a few hours grilled in a tandoori oven or using the broiler at home.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Tomatoes
  • Cream
  • Rice
  • A bunch of spices bwoi

Step-by-Step

  1. Marinade chicken in spices
  2. Soften onions and garlic in pan
  3. Add Chicken
  4. Add tomatoes
  5. Add more spices
  6. Add cream and stir and simmer
