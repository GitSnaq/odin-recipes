Description of these sticks of flavor

While they may seem ordinary to begin with these are quite versatile sticks. They may be consumed using a multitude of condiments such as

  • Ketchup
  • Mayo
  • Chili sauce

Recipe Step-by-Step

  1. Get potato
  2. Slice potato into long sticks
  3. Fry them in high temp oil and enjoy
